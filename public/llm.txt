# Let Solutions Technical Institute

Let Solutions is a premier technology training institute located in Tirur, Malappuram (Dist), Kerala, India. Established in 2012, the institute specializes in highly practical, career-oriented coaching in computer engineering, advanced chip-level hardware servicing, cybersecurity, and home/business automation systems.

## Core Curricula & Training Programs

* **Master Diploma In Chiplevel Engineering (MDCE)**
  * **Short Description**: The premium flagship program covering advanced component-level servicing.
  * **Detailed Modules**: Advanced electronics repair, SMD components, power section troubleshooting, circuit tracing, advanced fault diagnosis, BIOS programming, BGA rework & reballing, laptop/desktop motherboard diagnostics, and advanced smartphone hardware repair.
  * **Duration**: 6 months or 12 months options.
  * **Highlights**: Free toolkit, state-of-the-art laboratory access, 100% placement support, and structured completion certification.

* **Junior Penetration Tester**
  * **Short Description**: Fundamentals of ethical hacking and cyber defence.
  * **Detailed Modules**: Network security, vulnerability assessment, web application testing, active and passive reconnaissance, and real-case penetration testing simulation.
  * **Duration**: 6 months.

* **Electronics Technician**
  * **Short Description**: Practical foundational rules and board architecture of electronics components.
  * **Detailed Modules**: Component assembly, multimeter testing, soldering/desoldering techniques, PCB fundamentals, power supply servicing, and preventive maintenance.
  * **Duration**: 1 month.
