Our Story

LET SOLUTIONS is a professional technology training institute established in 2012, focused on practical and career-oriented education in computer engineering, cybersecurity, and chip-level servicing.

Our flagship program, the Master Diploma in Computer Engineering (MDCE), combines multiple technology domains into a single comprehensive career-focused course.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a leading technology training institute in India by empowering students with practical skills.

Mission: To provide high-quality, practical-oriented technical education through real-time training and advanced lab facilities.
